I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Warsaw, under the supervision of Prof. Przemysław Biecek and co-supervision of Dr. Matthew Tivnan. My research focuses on developing generative modeling techniques to construct novel explainability algorithms for computer vision models. My greatest passion lies in exploring advanced mathematical methodologies and translating them into scalable  practical use cases.

Currently, I serve as an AI Researcher and Focus Lead at the Centre for Credible AI. Recently, I completed a Visiting Researcher tenure at Harvard Medical School & Massachusetts General Hospital.
